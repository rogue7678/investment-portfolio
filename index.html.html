How This Simulation Works

No real money is ever used. Every figure on this page — your balance, profits, prices, and trades — is generated for practice. Nothing here connects to a real wallet or exchange.

The 7-day cycle mimics how short-term investment tracking feels in real markets: a countdown, fluctuating value, and a running profit or loss. When a cycle ends, you can reset it and try a different scenario.

Use the Adjust Investment button to see how a larger or smaller starting amount changes your simulated outcome, and watch the chart and risk meter respond.
